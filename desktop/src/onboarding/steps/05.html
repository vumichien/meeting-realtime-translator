Point Zoom or Meet at the cable

Open your meeting app audio settings and choose the virtual cable as the microphone. Keep your real mic selected inside Babel Mic.

Zoom: Settings → Audio → Microphone → CABLE Output or virtual source.

Google Meet: More options → Settings → Audio → Microphone → virtual source.
